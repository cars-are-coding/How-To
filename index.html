Tasty Buns

Ingredients

  • 4 cups. flour
  • 2(.25 ounces)packages. yeast
  • 1 cup. milk
  • 3/4 cup. water
  • 1/4 cup. Vegetable Oil
  • 1/4 cup. White Sugar
  • 1 teaspoon. Salt

Steps

  1. Stir 2 cups flour and yeasy together in a large bowl.
  2. Heat milk, water, oil, sugar, and salt together in a microwave-safe bowl in the micorowave until lukewarm; add to flour mixture and beat until smooth, about 3 minutes.
  3. Mix in the 2 cups of remaining flour, then add up to 1 cup more flour as necessary to make a soft bough.
  4. Dust a flat surface with flour; turn dough out onto floured surface and let rest under a bowl for about 10 minutes. Meanwhile, lightly grease a baking sheet.
  5. Shape dough into 12 slightly flat balls and place on the prepared baking sheet to rise until doubled in size.
  6. preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Bake in the preheted oven for 12 to 15 minutes.
  7. let cool and enjoy
